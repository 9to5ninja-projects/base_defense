# Design Notes

## Concept Art Analysis (2025-11-24)

### Layout
- **Game Field**: Left side of the screen.
  - 8x12 Grid for buildings.
  - "Implied" foundations showing 8 unlocked slots at the bottom.
- **UI / Info Panel**: Right side of the screen.
  - Split into sections (maybe 3).
  - One section dedicated to "Selected Building Info" during Build Mode.

### Mechanics
- **Enemy Spawning**: 
  - Spawns from the top of the screen at a random X coordinate.
- **Shields**:
  - Visual representation: Thickness = Max HP.
  - Appearance scales/shrinks as damage is taken.

### Grid
- 8 Columns (Slots).
- ~12 Rows (Vertical stacking limit).
